Step 1. Clone from github.
Step 2. Load repo in netbeans.
Step 3. Go to Tools->Ant Libraries.
Step 4. Click Add Library, name it LWJGL.
Step 5. Add slick.jar, lwjgl.jar, lwjgl_util.jar, and jinput.jar to the Classpath tab. (They can be found in the cloned repo)
Step 6. Add lwjgl_javadoc.jar to the Javadoc tab.
Step 7. Click OK.
Step 8. Right click libraries on the left side of the screen.
Step 9. Click add library, select LWJGL.
